Esquire Attire Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 5, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Esquire Attire ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and shares information in connection with our application and its integration with Third-Party Social Media Services.

Data Collection

Our application uses official developer interfaces to interact with Social Media Services. We may access public profile information and content metadata (such as board titles or image descriptions) that you have authorized us to access.

How We Use Data

Information retrieved from Social Media Services is used exclusively to provide the features of our application, such as content organization and scheduling. We do not store your login credentials.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your data to third parties. Data is only shared with Integrated Partners as necessary to perform the actions you trigger within the app.

Your Rights

You can revoke our access to your data at any time through the security settings of the Social Media Service you have connected to our app.
